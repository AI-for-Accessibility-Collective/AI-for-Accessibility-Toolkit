Carbon capture and storage technologies offer potential pathways for removing existing atmospheric carbon dioxide.

Individual actions also play a crucial role in combating climate change. Supporting policies and politicians committed to environmental protection helps drive systemic change. Education and awareness-raising efforts are essential for building the broad social consensus needed for transformative action.
